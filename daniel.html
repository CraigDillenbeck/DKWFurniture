Daniel's passion for building furniture blooms from a lifelong curiosity of 'how things work'. Continually keeping his hands dirty, Daniel learned at a very young age there is a tremendous learning experience with disassembling and reassembling all sorts of objects. Working with wood came naturally as has lived in the Western North Carolina mountains his entire life.

Honing his interests in high school in a wide range of crafting classes including woods, leathers, and clay lead him to enrolling in Appalachian State University's Furniture Manufacturing program, which he completed in 2005.
